Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact Inkyy Team?

You can reach us through our contact form on our website or by emailing us at hello@inkyy.com We typically respond within 24 hours.

What services do you offer?

We offer various digital services, including web design, development, and branding.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer ongoing website maintenance services to ensure that your website remains up to date, secure, and running smoothly. This includes regular updates, security checks, and content management.

How long does it take to design and develop a website?

The timeline for designing and developing a website varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. On average, it can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks, including the planning, design, development, and testing phases.

Do you require a deposit for projects?

Yes, we typically require a deposit before starting any project. This ensures both parties are committed to the project. The deposit amount is usually 50% of the total project cost, with the remaining balance due upon completion.
